Detailed Alternative Breakdowns

A closer look at how each tool compares for different use cases.

Client Koi vs. Instant Data Scraper

Instant Data Scraper is a popular free Chrome extension, but it's a general-purpose web scraper — not purpose-built for Google Maps. It can extract visible table data from any website, but it cannot visit linked business websites to find hidden email addresses. It also lacks CAPTCHA bypass, anti-ban mechanics, and social media extraction. If you only need basic name/address/phone data, Instant Data Scraper works. If you need emails, social links, and overnight capability, Client Koi is the clear winner.

Client Koi vs. Outscraper

Outscraper is a powerful cloud-based scraping service with excellent data quality. However, it starts at $29/month and charges per record for large volumes. For agencies scraping thousands of leads monthly, costs can quickly reach $100-$200. Client Koi provides comparable email extraction and social media scraping completely free. The trade-off: Outscraper runs in the cloud (no Chrome needed), while Client Koi runs locally in your browser.

Client Koi vs. Maps Scraper

Maps Scraper (also known as Google Maps Data Extractor) offers a free tier with limited exports and a paid tier for full access. It extracts basic business information but does not perform deep website scraping for hidden emails or social media profiles. Client Koi's deep scraping engine visits each business's actual website, giving you significantly more contact data points per lead — at zero cost.
