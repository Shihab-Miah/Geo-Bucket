Pro Tips: Get More Leads
with Client Koi

10 expert b2b lead generation strategies to maximize your Google Maps results. Learn how to get b2b leads for free and apply the best cold email outreach tips.

1

Clean Your Data After Every Session

After exporting your leads, remove duplicate entries and invalid email addresses before starting outreach. Use a free tool like Hunter.io Email Verifier or NeverBounce to validate emails — this keeps your bounce rate low and protects your sender reputation.

2

Use Specific, Long-Tail Search Tags

Instead of broad searches like "restaurants", use highly specific tags: "Italian restaurants with delivery in Brooklyn NY". Specific searches produce fewer but significantly higher-quality leads — businesses that exactly match your target customer profile.

3

Set Smart Delay Intervals

Configure Client Koi's delay jitter to 3-7 seconds between actions for regular use, or 5-10 seconds for overnight sessions. Random delays mimic human browsing patterns and dramatically reduce the chance of triggering Google's bot detection systems.

4

Run Overnight Extraction Sessions

Enable 2Captcha integration, set conservative delay intervals, and start Client Koi before bed. By morning, you'll have thousands of verified leads — emails, phone numbers, websites, and social links — all ready for export without any manual work.

5

Target Unclaimed Business Listings

Businesses listed on Google Maps without a website are often the highest-value prospects for digital marketing agencies. They need online presence services the most. Filter your exported data to find entries with phone numbers but no website URL.

6

Export and Segment Immediately

Don't wait to organize your data. After each scraping session, export to CSV and immediately segment leads by city, niche, and quality signals (review count, rating, has website). Pre-segmented lists make personalized outreach dramatically easier.

7

Combine Scraping with Cold Email

Import your Client Koi exports into cold email tools like Lemlist, Instantly, or Mailchimp. Use the business name and category data for personalization: "Hi [Business Name], I noticed you're a [category] in [city]..." — personalized emails get 3x higher response rates.

8

Use Multiple Chrome Profiles

For large-scale scraping across many cities or niches, create separate Chrome profiles (each with its own Google account). Run Client Koi in one profile while you work in another. This distributes your scraping activity and reduces risk per account.

9

Keep Client Koi Updated

Google occasionally updates the Maps interface (DOM structure), which can break scraping selectors. We push fixes to the GitHub repository immediately when this happens. Re-download the latest version monthly to stay compatible.

10

Verify Emails Before Sending

Sending to invalid emails damages your domain reputation and can get you blacklisted. Use free verification services like Hunter.io, ZeroBounce, or Email Hippo to validate your extracted email list before launching any outreach campaign. Aim for a bounce rate under 3%.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about B2B lead generation with Client Koi.

Is Client Koi safe for my Chrome account?

Yes. Client Koi operates locally in your browser and uses humanized delay intervals, randomized mouse movements, and session rotation to prevent your account from being flagged by Google's anti-bot systems.

Does it find hidden email addresses?

Yes. Google Maps rarely displays email addresses directly. Client Koi visits the business's linked website in the background to extract contact emails, phone numbers, and social media profiles that aren't visible on the Maps listing itself.

What if Google updates their Maps code?

We actively monitor Google Maps DOM changes. When structural updates occur, we push new stable releases to the GitHub repository immediately. Simply re-download the latest version to stay compatible.

How do I handle the Manifest V3 warning?

When running an unpacked extension in Developer Mode, Chrome may display a harmless warning about Service Workers or Manifest V3. You can safely ignore this notification — it does not affect the extension's functionality.

Can I run Client Koi overnight?

Absolutely! Enable 2Captcha integration and set a conservative delay interval (5-8 seconds between actions). Client Koi will automatically solve CAPTCHAs and continue scraping while you sleep, yielding thousands of leads by morning.

Is scraping Google Maps legal?

Scraping publicly available data from Google Maps is generally considered legal. The US court case hiQ Labs v. LinkedIn (2022) established that scraping publicly accessible data does not violate the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act. Always use reasonable rate limits and comply with GDPR/CCPA when reaching out to contacts.
