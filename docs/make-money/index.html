Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about making money with Client Koi B2B leads.

How much money can I make as a beginner?

Most beginners close their first AI website sale ($300–$500) within 7 days by pitching 20 businesses with no website. By consistently sending 30 messages a day, reaching $2,000/month is achievable within 30–60 days.

Do I need coding skills to build websites for clients?

No! Tools like v0.dev, Framer AI, and Hostinger AI generate complete production-ready websites from simple text prompts. You only need to customize text, colors, and business details.

Is selling B2B lead datasets legal?

Yes. Compiling and organizing publicly available business data (business names, public phone numbers, addresses, websites, public emails) is 100% legal under global digital commerce laws.

How do I pitch business owners without sounding spammy?

Always provide value first! Instead of saying "buy my website", send a 5-second video or live link showing a pre-made mockup of their website or an audit of their missing reviews. Value-first outreach gets 5x higher response rates.
