Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about B2B lead generation with Client Koi.

Is this safe for my Chrome account?

Yes. Client Koi operates locally in your browser environment and uses humanized delay intervals to prevent your session from being flagged by Google's anti-bot systems.

Does it find hidden emails?

Google Maps rarely displays email addresses directly. Our engine visits the business's linked website in the background to extract contact emails and social media profiles.

What if Google updates their Maps code?

We actively monitor Google Maps DOM changes. When structural updates occur, we push new stable releases to this repository immediately.

How do I handle the Manifest V3 warning?

When running an unpacked extension in Developer Mode, Chrome may display a harmless warning about Service Workers or Manifest V3. You can safely ignore this.
